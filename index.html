OUR STORY

MORE THAN JUST A SALON

For over four decades, Total E'Clips has been a part of the Smithfield community. What started as a small salon on Center Street has grown into a place where generations of families come to look and feel their best.

Our stylists, including Wendy, Sherri, and April, are known for making every client feel like a friend the moment they walk through the door. That warmth is something you just can't fake.

Whether you need a quick trim, a bold new color, or a fresh set of nails, you will always leave with a smile.
